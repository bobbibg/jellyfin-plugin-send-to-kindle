My Kindle

Set the email address Jellyfin should deliver books to. Find yours at amazon.com → Manage Your Content and Devices → Preferences → Personal Document Settings. It'll look like your-name_abc123@kindle.com.

You also need to add the server's "From" address (set by your admin) to your Approved Personal Document E-mail List on the same page, otherwise Amazon will reject the books.

Must end in @kindle.com or @free.kindle.com.
